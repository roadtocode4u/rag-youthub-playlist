Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How many paid leaves do employees get?
A: Employees receive 24 paid leaves per year as per company policy.

Q: What is the notice period for resignation?
A: The standard notice period is 60 days.

Q: Can I work from home?
A: Yes, employees can work remotely for up to 2 days per week.

Q: What are the working hours?
A: The working hours are from 9:30 AM to 6:30 PM.
